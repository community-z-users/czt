\subsection{Informal argument}

To typeset an informal argument, you write in \LaTeX\
\begin{verbatim}
\begin{argue}
    S \dres (T \dres R) \\
\t1     = \id S \comp \id T \comp R \\
\t1     = \id (S \cap T) \comp R & law about $\id$ \\
\t1	= (S \cap T) \dres R.
\end{argue}
\end{verbatim}
%
which corresponds to
%
\begin{argue}
    S \dres (T \dres R) \\
\t1     = \id S \comp \id T \comp R \\
\t1     = \id (S \cap T) \comp R & law about $\id$ \\
\t1	= (S \cap T) \dres R.
\end{argue}
